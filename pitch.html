The Problem

Owning an EV without a driveway is a nightmare

Public infrastructure is expensive, unreliable, and out of the way. Meanwhile millions of driveways sit idle with chargers attached.

🏢
60% of EV owners rent
In the UK, the majority of EV owners live in flats or rented homes with no private parking and no way to install a home charger.
💸
Public charging costs 3× more
Rapid chargers cost 65-85p per kWh. Home charging costs 28p. The gap is enormous — and falls hardest on those who can least afford it.
🔌
Idle chargers everywhere
Over 500,000 home chargers sit unused for 20+ hours a day. Hosts want to monetise them. There's no simple, trusted way to do it.

The Solution

Airbnb for EV chargers

NestCharge connects EV drivers with nearby hosts who have a spare charger. Book in seconds, charge like you're home, pay automatically.

1
Driver opens the NestCharge PWA Sees a map of verified chargers within walking distance. No app download required — works instantly in any mobile browser.
2
Books a time slot and pays Stripe handles payment securely. Driver gets a booking reference and access instructions after confirmation.
3
Shows up and charges Host is notified. Driver plugs in. NestCharge monitors the session and notifies both parties when charging is complete.
4
Host earns passively 80% of every session goes straight to the host. Average host earns £600–£900/year with zero ongoing effort.
